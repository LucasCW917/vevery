Origins

A language born from music

Vevery takes its name from its own word for notes — a self-referential act that defines its character. Designed to be soft, flowing, and musical in its feel, every word in Vevery is built from a set of root concepts that combine like instruments in an ensemble.

Where most languages accumulate words through history and accident, Vevery builds them deliberately — compounding roots the way German does, but with a phonology drawn from vowel-rich, gentle sounds. To speak Vevery is to compose.

𝄞
Musical Roots
Born from a notation system. Every concept in Vevery flows from a small set of expressive roots.
◈
Compound Logic
Words are built by combining roots. woraaeve — word-store — means dictionary. Once you know the roots, you can read the language.
∿
No Tenses
Time in Vevery is expressed explicitly — three days before, one day after. The verb stays pure, uncontorted by conjugation.
⟁
Free Word Order
Subject-Verb-Object is the default, but the speaker's voice shapes the sentence. Grammar serves expression, not the other way around.
